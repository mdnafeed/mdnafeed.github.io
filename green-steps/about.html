About Us

Greensteps is started with an aim to improve resource recovery from dry waste. We run a specialized material recovery facility that produces top quality material outputs from mixed dry waste.

We collect segregated dry waste from source, transport it to our mechanized facility, segregate it and dispatch it to the authorized end destinations. We not only promote recycling and reuse of recovered material, but also divert enormous amount of waste from the landfills.

Our Aim

Our aim is to see continuous growth not only in business, but also in aspects of knowledge, experience, and sustainable awareness; taking full consideration of our actions and its impact on the community as a whole.

Our Vision

To become industry leaders in smart waste management solutions through innovative and efficient technology that will ensure the sustainability of our future.

Our ambition

Our ambition- To help safeguard resources, change inefficient processes, nurture new business models and increase recycling.

Our Mission

To deliver sustainable and innovative waste management services so our economy and our planet can thrive as one.
